# Bank of Africa — Customer Handbook

## 1. Account Types

### Savings Account
- Minimum opening balance: NGN 1,000
- Interest rate: 4.5% per annum (calculated daily, paid monthly)
- Daily transaction limit: NGN 200,000 (Tier 2), Unlimited (Tier 3)
- No monthly maintenance fee
- Free first 3 transfers per month; NGN 50 per additional transfer

### Current Account
- Minimum opening balance: NGN 10,000
- No interest earned on balances
- Daily transaction limit: NGN 500,000 (Tier 2), Unlimited (Tier 3)
- Monthly maintenance fee: NGN 500 (waived if average monthly balance exceeds NGN 100,000)
- Comes with a debit card (AfriGo or Visa)

### Fixed Deposit Account
- Minimum deposit: NGN 100,000
- Tenors: 30, 60, 90, 180, or 365 days
- Interest rates: 8% (30 days), 10% (90 days), 12% (180 days), 14% (365 days)
- Early withdrawal penalty: Forfeiture of 50% of accrued interest
- Interest paid at maturity or rolled over automatically

### Credit Line Account
- Available to Tier 3 customers with salary accounts
- Credit limit: Up to 3x monthly salary
- Interest rate: 2.5% per month on outstanding balance
- Minimum monthly repayment: 10% of outstanding balance
- Late payment fee: NGN 5,000

## 2. Transaction Fees

### Transfers
- Internal transfers (Bank of Africa to Bank of Africa): Free
- NIP transfers (to other banks via NIBSS): NGN 50 for amounts up to NGN 50,000; NGN 100 for NGN 50,001 - NGN 1,000,000; NGN 250 for above NGN 1,000,000
- International transfers: 1% of transfer amount + NGN 2,500 processing fee
- USSD transfers (*894#): NGN 10 per transaction

### ATM Withdrawals
- Bank of Africa ATMs: Free (first 5 per month), NGN 35 thereafter
- Other bank ATMs: NGN 65 per withdrawal
- Daily ATM withdrawal limit: NGN 100,000
- Weekly withdrawal limit: NGN 500,000 (individuals), NGN 5,000,000 (corporates)

### POS Transactions
- POS purchases: Free (no charge to cardholder)
- POS cash withdrawal: NGN 100 per transaction

### Card Fees
- Debit card issuance: NGN 1,000 (AfriGo), NGN 1,500 (Visa)
- Card replacement: NGN 1,500
- Annual card maintenance: NGN 1,000

## 3. KYC Tiers and Limits

### Tier 1 (Basic)
- Requirements: Phone number + BVN verification
- Daily transaction limit: NGN 50,000
- Maximum account balance: NGN 300,000
- No international transfers

### Tier 2 (Standard)
- Requirements: BVN + NIN + valid ID document + address verification
- Daily transaction limit: NGN 200,000
- Maximum account balance: NGN 5,000,000
- International transfers: Up to USD 2,000 per transaction

### Tier 3 (Enhanced)
- Requirements: All Tier 2 + utility bill + employer verification + enhanced due diligence
- No daily transaction limit
- No maximum account balance
- International transfers: Unlimited (subject to CBN regulations)
- Access to credit products

## 4. Dispute Resolution

### Process
1. Report the dispute through the banking app, by calling +234 1 234 5678, or visiting any branch
2. You will receive a dispute reference number within 24 hours
3. The bank will investigate within 14 working days (as required by CBN)
4. If the dispute is in your favor, reimbursement will be processed within 48 hours
5. If you disagree with the resolution, you may escalate to the CBN Consumer Protection Department

### Fraud Reporting
- Report unauthorized transactions immediately by calling our 24/7 fraud hotline: +234 1 234 5679
- You must report fraud within 72 hours to qualify for guaranteed reimbursement under CBN APP fraud guidelines
- The bank will freeze the affected account within 30 minutes of receiving a fraud report
- Investigation will be completed within 14 working days

## 5. Digital Banking Services

### Mobile App Features
- Account balance and transaction history
- Fund transfers (internal and interbank)
- Bill payments (electricity, DSTV, internet, airtime)
- Card management (freeze/unfreeze, set limits)
- Savings goals and automated savings
- QR code payments

### USSD Banking (*894#)
- Available on all phone types (no internet required)
- Check balance, transfer funds, buy airtime
- Transaction limit: NGN 50,000 per transaction

### Internet Banking
- Full account management via web browser
- Bulk transfers and batch payments
- Statement downloads (PDF, CSV)
- Standing orders and direct debits
